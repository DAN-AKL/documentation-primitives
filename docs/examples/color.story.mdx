# Color

We use color in meaningful ways in all expressions of our brand. Our colors are optimized to meet WCAG 2.0 Level AA Accessibility Guidelines.

## Primary palette

Our primary palette has four colors: yellow, black, grey and white. Yellow is friendly, vibrant colour that draws interest. Black, grey and white reflect simplicity and convey confidence.

<Swatches>
  <Swatch name="Yellow" value="#FECC00" />
  <Swatch name="Black" value="#000000" />
  <Swatch name="Grey" value="#53565A" />
  <Swatch name="White" value="#FFFFFF" />
</Swatches>

## Secondary palette

The secondary colors - blue and coral - are fun, friendly extensions to the primary colour palette. Grey is used to add depth and variation for backgrounds, and illustrations. The secondary palette is used for backgrounds, illustrations, text and icons and helps us convey a human touch, vibrancy and energy.

  <Swatch name="Coral" value="#F16B53" />

</Swatches>

The extended palette consists of shades of colors from the primary and secondary palettes.
