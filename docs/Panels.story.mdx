## Options

### Prefix

By default, the context is prefixed by **"Note:"** which could be changed via the `prefix` prop.

<Preview>
  <Story name="prefix">
    <Info prefix="ProTip">
      Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod
      tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.
    </Info>
  </Story>
</Preview>
